V6.5.2 Stable Release

⟺ Dimension — click 2 points to annotate distance. Toggle m / ft″ in the dim bar.

Layers — left panel. Add layers, toggle visibility, assign via Properties → Active Layer.

✂ Section — slice the model on X/Y/Z with a live position slider.

Door/Window — auto-cuts a real opening through walls on placement.

⬡ Chamfer / Fillet — click edge 1 (highlighted), click edge 2, type distance/radius, Enter. All four tools use this two-edge workflow.

⬡ HChamfer flat diagonal cut on two horizontal edges. ◌ HFillet true quarter-circle arc on two horizontal edges. Both edges highlight before you enter a value.

E edge toggle — fully clears all edges including after geometry edits. Snaps are now OFF by default; enable individually in the Snapping panel.
