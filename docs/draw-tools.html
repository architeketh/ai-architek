Draw Tools

All 19 draw tools available in AI-Architek V6 — with keyboard shortcuts and usage notes.

How to use: Click a tool in the left panel or press its keyboard shortcut. Then click in the viewport to place points. Press Esc or double-click to finish drawing.

Shapes & Primitives

ToolKeyHow it works
RectangleRClick two corners to define the rectangle. Can be extruded into a box.
SquareQLike Rectangle but constrained to equal sides.
CircleCClick center, drag to set radius. Creates a circle or an extruded cylinder.
ConeNTwo clicks define the base, then an interactive height bar sets the tip.
PyramidYSquare base pyramid — click two corners for the base, then set height.
Sphere V6EFull ellipsoid. Drag to set width, then adjust height. Height scales the Y axis independently.
Dome V6—Upper hemisphere with a flat base cap. Great for domed roofs and vaults.

Lines & Curves

ToolKeyHow it works
LineLMulti-point polyline. Click to place each vertex. Can be extruded into a wall or slab.
Curved LineJCatmull-Rom spline — smooth curve through your clicked points.
PolygonPClosed polygon. Click multiple points, then close the shape. Extrudable.
Curved PolyUSmooth closed spline polygon — like Polygon but with rounded edges.

Architectural Elements

ToolKeyHow it works
WallWClick to chain connected wall segments at any angle. Double-click or Esc to finish.
DoorDClick two points on the floor plan, or click a wall face to auto-place a door opening.
WindowIClick two points on the floor plan, or click a wall face to auto-place a window opening.
FloorFMulti-point closed polygon that extrudes into a floor slab.
CeilingGCeiling panel — same as Floor but placed at ceiling height.
RoofOClick a building footprint to generate a hip or gable roof automatically.

Face Drawing

ToolKeyHow it works
Face RectBDraw a rectangle directly on the surface of any existing face. Useful for adding panels and openings.
Face CircleHDraw a circle directly on any face. Combine with Boolean Subtract to punch circular holes.
Snapping: All draw tools use the snapping system. Endpoint snap finds corners, midpoint snap finds edge centers, and grid snap keeps things aligned. Toggle snapping on/off in the toolbar.
