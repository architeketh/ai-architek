// What's inside

Everything in one file.

18+ draw tools, full transform suite, real exports — runs entirely in your browser. No install. No account. No internet after download.

🏗️

Architectural Draw Tools

Walls, doors, windows, floors, ceilings, roofs, and 10+ shape primitives including spheres, domes, and cones — all with keyboard shortcuts.

📐

Precision Transforms

3-step Move & Copy, Mirror, Rotate (X/Y/Z axis), Scale, Stretch, Extrude Face, Boolean Union/Subtract, Chamfer, and Fillet on any edge.

📷

Named Camera System

Save unlimited named camera views. Interactive frustum cones let you drag eye/target points directly in the scene. Per-camera lens cycling and visibility toggle.

🎯

Smart Snapping

Endpoint, midpoint, grid, and angle snapping with a generous 50px threshold. Vertical snap in elevation views with ortho-axis constraint to prevent depth drift.

☀️

Solar Lighting & Sky

Real azimuth/elevation calculations for 14 regions by month and time of day. Upload any panoramic JPG/PNG as a sky dome — shows in elevation views too.

📦

5 Export Formats

Export to DXF (Rhino-compatible), OBJ with normals, binary 3DS, PDF/PNG viewport capture, and native JSON for full scene save/reload.

// Changelog

What's new in V6.

Major additions since V3 — new shapes, camera system, sky, edge tools, and a full round of bug fixes.

New

Sphere & Dome E

Full ellipsoid sphere with Y-axis height scaling, plus a dome primitive with a flat base cap — both fully extrudable and exportable.

New

Camera Frustum Cones

Save named camera views with lens settings. Yellow wireframe cones show each camera's FOV in the scene — drag the eye or target spheres to reposition live.

New

Sky System

Sky on/off toggle with color + horizon pickers. Upload any JPG/PNG panorama as a full sky dome — it wraps equirectangularly and appears in all elevation views.

New

Chamfer & Fillet

Click any vertical edge and type a bevel distance (Chamfer) or arc radius (Fillet). Each corner can be filleted independently and all operations fully undo.

New

Left & Back Views

Two new elevation views — Left opposite of 6 and Back — completing the full 6-direction orthographic set alongside Front, Right, Top, and Iso.

New

Tumble Mode T

Free spherical orbit around the model center — rotate to any angle including below the floor plane. Great for checking underside geometry.

New

Two-Pass Selection

Exact geometry ray-cast first, then a 24px proximity fallback for thin or small objects — so you never miss a click on complex scenes.

Fixed

Save/Load Position Bug

Boolean and stretched objects now reload at correct XYZ positions. The previous double-Y-offset that moved objects on reload is resolved.

Fixed

Copy & Fillet Fixes

Copy/mirror of stretched objects now clones live geometry. Fillet no longer overwrites previous fillets, and Chamfer/Fillet undo restores geometry instead of deleting the object.
