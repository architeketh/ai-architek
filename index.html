// What's inside

Built for real work.

Everything you need to bring your 3D ideas to life, without the bloat.

⬡

Polygon Mesh Editing

Full suite of mesh editing tools including extrude, bevel, loop cuts, and vertex manipulation.

◈

Real-time Preview

See your changes instantly with a live OpenGL viewport that keeps up with every edit.

⬢

Export Anywhere

Export to OBJ, FBX, STL, GLTF, and more. Compatible with every major pipeline.

◉

Procedural Tools

Non-destructive modifiers let you experiment freely and iterate without losing your work.

◎

Lightweight

Runs fast on modest hardware. No subscription. No cloud required. Just install and create.

⬦

Open File Format

Projects save in a readable format so your work is always yours — no lock-in.
